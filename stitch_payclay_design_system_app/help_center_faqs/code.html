Popular FAQs

How do I reset my clay wallet PIN? expand_more
To reset your PIN, go to Settings > Security > Change PIN. You'll need to verify your identity via biometrics or email code before creating a new 6-digit soft-key PIN.
Can I use PayClay for international transfers? expand_more
Yes! PayClay supports over 50 currencies with real-time exchange rates and minimal fees. Just tap 'Send', select the destination country, and we'll handle the rest.
What is the "Soft Shield" protection? expand_more
Soft Shield is our proprietary AI-driven security layer that monitors transactions for unusual patterns. If we detect anything suspicious, we temporarily pause the payment and ask for your clay-tap confirmation.
Are there fees for adding money? expand_more
Adding money via bank transfer is always free. Card top-ups may incur a small processing fee depending on your card issuer and region.
