How to reset my UPI PIN?

1

Open Payment Settings

Launch the PayClay app and navigate to your profile by tapping the avatar in the top right. Select Bank Accounts from the list to see your linked accounts.

2

Select Your Bank

Tap on the specific bank account you wish to reset the PIN for. Look for the "Forget UPI PIN" option located usually at the bottom of the account details screen.

3

Verify & Reset

Enter the last 6 digits and expiry date of your Debit Card. Once verified via OTP, you can set your new 4 or 6-digit UPI PIN. Confirm it once more to complete the process.

lightbulb

Pro Tip

Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from PayClay or your bank. We will never ask for these details.
